Gas leakage from the nozzle

X19 (2019) — English translation

The rocket moves due to jet thrust generated by the release of gas heated to a very high temperature. In the combustion chamber of the rocket (at the entrance to the nozzle), hot gas of high pressure $p_1$ and temperature $T_1$ is constantly formed. Having passed through the nozzle, the gas flies through a narrow hole into the atmosphere, where the pressure is $p_2$. The molar mass of the gas is $\mu$. Consider the process of gas flow in the nozzle to be equilibrium and adiabatic.

Find the gas temperature $T_2$ at the nozzle exit
