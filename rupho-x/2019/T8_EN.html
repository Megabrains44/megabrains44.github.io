Crooked cylinder

X19 (2019) — English translation

A cylindrical vessel of height $H=50~\text{cm}$ with mirror walls is filled with a transparent liquid, the refractive index of which decreases with height according to a linear law. Near the bottom of the cylinder there is a point light source with power $P=100~\text{Вт}$. The refractive index of the liquid near the bottom is $n_1=1.50$, and at the surface $n_2=1.44$. The bottom of the vessel and its lid are covered with a substance that absorbs all radiation that hits it
