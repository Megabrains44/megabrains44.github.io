X19 (2019) — English translation

At sunset, the hare noticed a fox in the field and began to run away from it at a speed of $v$. But the fox was blind and began to catch up not with the hare, but with the end of his shadow at the same speed $v$. First, the fox, the hare and the end of his shadow form a regular triangle with side $L$.

A1
What distance $l$ will be established between the hare and the fox after some long time (not enough for the sun to go down and lengthen the shadow)?
