The “cunning” source provides constant power $P$ in the external circuit.

\problembox{A1}{Find the current through the source and the voltage across it as a function of time, if it is connected to a resistor with resistance $R$.}{0.50}

\problembox{A2}{Find the current through the source and the voltage across it depending on time, if it is connected to a capacitor of capacity $C$.}{0.50}

Now a circuit is connected to the “cunning” source, the diagram of which is shown in the figure. $R=5~\Omega$, $P=8~\text{W}$, diode opening voltage $U_0=2~\text{V}$.
