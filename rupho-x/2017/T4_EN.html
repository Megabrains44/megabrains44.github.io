Two cycles

X17 (2017) — English translation

The diagram in coordinates ($\rho$, $T$) shows two cycles (see figure), according to which heat engines I and II operate. They use helium as a working fluid. These cyclic processes have efficiency factors $\eta_1$ and $\eta_2$, satisfying the condition: $(3−\eta_1)(1−\eta_2)=1$.
