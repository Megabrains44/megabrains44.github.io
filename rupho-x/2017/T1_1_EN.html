Rod, thread and bead

X17 (2017) — English translation

A rod of mass $M$ and length $L$ is suspended by its ends on long identical threads fixed at point $O$. The threads are threaded through a small bead of mass $m$, which can move along them without friction (see figure). The acceleration due to gravity is $g$.

Fig. 1.
A1
Determine the angle $\alpha_0$ between the thread and the vertical for the case when the system is in equilibrium.
1.40
A2
The bead is moved a short distance down and released. Find the period of small oscillations of the system around the equilibrium position (the movement of the bead and the rod occurs along a vertical straight line).
5.60
