\title{\textbf{Rod, thread and bead}}
\author{\normalsize X17 (2017) --- English translation}
\date{}
\begin{document}
\maketitle

A rod of mass $M$ and length $L$ is suspended by its ends on long identical threads fixed at point $O$. The threads are threaded through a small bead of mass $m$, which can move along them without friction (see figure). The acceleration due to gravity is $g$.

\begin{figure}[H]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.72\textwidth]{T1_1_EN_assets/fig1.jpg}
    \caption{Figure 1.}
\end{figure}

\problembox{A1}{Determine the angle $\alpha_0$ between the thread and the vertical for the case when the system is in equilibrium.}{1.40}

\problembox{A2}{The bead is moved a short distance down and released. Find the period of small oscillations of the system around the equilibrium position (the movement of the bead and the rod occurs along a vertical straight line).}{5.60}
