\title{\textbf{Close lenses}}
\author{\normalsize X21 (2021) --- English translation}
\date{}
\begin{document}
\maketitle

The optical system consists of a large number of collecting lenses with a focal length $F$ with a common main optical axis. The centers of adjacent lenses are at the same distance $d\ll{F}$ from each other, and the thickness of the lens can be neglected compared to $d$. The ray, before refraction in the center of the first lens, intersects the main optical axis at an angle of ${\alpha}\ll{1}$, and immediately after refraction in the last one, it intersects the main optical axis at the same angle.
