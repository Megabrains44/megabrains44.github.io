Heat engine

X21 (2021) — English translation

The Carnot heat engine operates with two heat reservoirs of finite heat capacity $C=500~\text{Дж/К}$. which at the initial moment of time are at temperatures $T_h=625~\text{К}$ and $T_c=298~\text{К}$. The power developed by a heat engine decreases exponentially with time $P=P_0\,\mathrm{e}^{-kt}$, where $P_0=960~\text{Вт}$ is the power at the initial moment of time, and $t$ is the time elapsed since the initial moment.
