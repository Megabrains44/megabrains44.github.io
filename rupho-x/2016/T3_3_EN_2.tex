\title{\textbf{Hill and depression}}
\author{\normalsize X16 (2016) --- English translation}
\date{}
\begin{document}
\maketitle

Bodies $A$ and $B$ move along adjacent paths, the length of which ascent $L$ is equal to the length of descent. The height $h$ of the slide and the depth $h$ of the depression are such that $h \ll L$. The initial velocities of $v_0$ bodies are identical and satisfy the condition: $v_0^2 \gg gh$.
