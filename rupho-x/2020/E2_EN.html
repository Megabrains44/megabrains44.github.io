Unknown plate

X20 (2020) — English translation

Equipment: plate with unknown dimensions, pin, clamp, stopwatch, block (as a ruler without divisions).

Fig. 1.
A1
There are holes in the middle of the homogeneous plate. They are located at equal distances from each other (and from the borders, see fig). Determine the width of the plate $b$ and the length of the plate $c$ as accurately as possible. For measurements, use only the measuring instruments specified in the equipment. Gravity acceleration $g=9.8~\text{m/s}^2$.
