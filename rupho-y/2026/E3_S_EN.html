B3Show that $v^2 = \gamma_1 h$ is in the slow rotation range and $v^2 = \gamma_2 h + b$ is in the fast rotation range.0.60
Answer: The frictional force is equivalent to an increase in the moment of inertia of all rotating parts by $\frac{1}{\alpha}$ times. Then we can write the ZSE: $$\frac{I\omega^2}{2\alpha} + \frac{m_0 v^2}{2} + const= m_0gh$$since $v = \omega R$ $$v^2 + const = \gamma h $$In the case of медленного вращения $v(h\! =\! 0) = 0$, therefore $const = 0$. Then можем записать for медленного and быстрого вращения соответственно: $$v^2 = \gamma_1h$$$$v^2 = \gamma_2 h+ b $$
B4Express the coefficients $\gamma_1$ and $\gamma_2$ in terms of $m$, $m_{0}$, $x_0$, $L$, $\delta$, $g$, $\alpha,$, $R$, $I_B$ and $I_1$.0.80

Answer: $$\gamma_1 = \frac{2\alpha m_0 g R^2}{\alpha m_0R^2 + I_B + I_1 + mx_0^2}$$$$\gamma_2 = \frac{2\alpha m_0 g R^2}{\alpha m_0R^2 + I_B + I_1 + m(\frac{L}{2} - \delta)^2}$$
