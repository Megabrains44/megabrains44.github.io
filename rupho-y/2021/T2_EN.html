Part A. Hole

A plane monochromatic wave with wavelength $\lambda$ and intensity $I_0$ is incident on a round hole of radius $R_0$ in an opaque plane parallel to the axis of the hole. Behind the plane there is a screen at a distance $L$. Consider $\frac{ \sqrt{\lambda L}}{R_0} \gtrsim 1$.

A1
Estimate the radius of the spot $R$ on the screen.
0.20
A2
Estimate the intensity $I$ at the center of the spot.
0.20
A3
Estimate the minimum achievable spot radius $R_{\text{min}}$ when varying the hole radius.
0.30

Further, the intensity of the maximum should be understood as the intensity of a plane wave propagating in the direction of the maximum with number $h$.
