Complex oscillatory system

Y13 (2013) — English translation

The mass $m_1$ is connected by threads to two identical masses $m_2$. The threads are thrown through small light blocks, the distance between which is $L$.

Fig. 1.
A1
At what mass ratio $m_1/m_2$ will the system be in a stable equilibrium position (mass loads $m_2$ do not touch the blocks)?

A2
Find the period of small oscillations of such a system (the mass $m_1$ oscillates along a vertical straight line).
