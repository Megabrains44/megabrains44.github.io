Rocket flight

Y16 (2016) — English translation

A space rocket rotates in a circular orbit at a distance $R=2.0\cdot{10^7}~\text{м}$ from the center of the Earth in the plane of the Earth’s orbit. Earth mass $m=6.00\cdot{10^{24}}~\text{kg}$, Sun mass $M=2.0\cdot{10^{30}}~\text{kg}$, Earth orbital radius $R_0=1.5\cdot{10^{11}}~\text{м}$, gravitational constant $G=6.67\cdot{10^{-11}}(\text{Н}\cdot{\text{м}^2})/\text{kg}^2$.

A1
Find the minimum value of the change in speed $\Delta{v}$ of the rocket, which may be sufficient for its departure from orbit outside the Solar System.
5.00
