\title{\textbf{Stopping ball}}
\author{\normalsize Y17 (2017) --- English translation}
\date{}
\begin{document}
\maketitle

A ball of radius $R$ is spun around its axis to an angular velocity $\omega_0$. The ball is placed in a hole of radius $r_1 < R$ and it stops in time $T_1$. The coefficient of friction between the ball and the hole is $\mu$.

\problembox{A1}{In what time $T_2$ will the ball stop with the same initial angular velocity if it is placed in a hole of radius $r_2 < R$?}{3.00}
