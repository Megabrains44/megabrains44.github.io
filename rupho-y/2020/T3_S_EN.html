A1The system has one degree of freedom, angle $\varphi$, and the position of the pendulum can be described by a function of this angle. Express the body's Cartesian coordinates $x(t)$ and $y(t)$ as functions of angle $\varphi(t)$ and time.0.50
Answer: $$x(t)=l\sin\varphi $$ $$y(t)=a\cos{\omega}t-l\cos\varphi $$
A2Find the velocity components $v_x$ and $v_y$ of the body. Express your answer in terms of angular velocity $\frac{d\varphi}{dt}$ and time.0.50
Answer: $$v_x=\frac{dx}{dt}=l\cos{\varphi}\frac{d\varphi}{dt} $$ $$v_y=\frac{dy}{dt}=l\sin{\varphi}\frac{d\varphi}{dt}-{\omega}a\sin{\omega}t $$
