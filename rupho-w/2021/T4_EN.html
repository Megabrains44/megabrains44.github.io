Hexagonal plate current

W21 (2021) — English translation

An electric current flows through the vertices $A,B,C$ and $D$ of a thin metal regular hexagon. The current strengths $2I$ and $5I$ flow into the vertices $A$ and $B$, respectively, and the current strength $13I$ flows out of the vertex $D$. The $OY$ axis is directed vertically upward, and the $OX$ axis is directed vertically to the left.

Fig. 1.
A1
Indicate the signs of the electric field projections $E_x$ and $E_y$ at the center of the plate, and also find the ratio of their magnitudes.
