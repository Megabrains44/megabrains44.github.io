\title{\textbf{Nonlinear element}}
\author{\normalsize W21 (2021) --- English translation}
\date{}
\begin{document}
\maketitle

The electrical circuit consists of a constant voltage source $U_0$, a capacitor of capacitance $C$, a nonlinear element NE, and a switch $K$. The current-voltage characteristic of the nonlinear element is as follows: $$\left(\frac{I}{I_0}\right)^2+\left(\frac{U}{U_0}\right)^2=1$$ for $I>0$ and $I=0$ for $|U|\geq U_0$ (see figure). The value $I_0$ is known. At the initial moment, the voltage across the capacitor is $U_0/2$. The switch $K$ is closed.

    \caption{Figure 1.}
\end{figure}

\problembox{A1}{Find the maximum power $P_1$ released on the nonlinear element.}{2.00}

\problembox{A2}{Find the maximum rate of change of energy of the capacitor $P_2$ and the time after closing the switch $\Delta t$ after which it is achieved.}{8.00}
